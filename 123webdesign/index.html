What is Elden Ring?

Elden Ring is an action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. The game is presented through third-person perspective, and the player may freely roam in its interactive free form exploration open world. Gameplay elements include fights, looting, horseback riding, interacting with non-player characters, and maintaining the character's status through levelling up, enhancing equipment and weapons, and learning spells.
